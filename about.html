Flavor Factory represents the scoring work of Alex Epton and Ryan Sciaino.




Better known for their experience in the studio and live music space, they have also scored commercial work for Puma, Facebook, and Getty Images among other brands. They are founding members of the band WIN WIN who saw their third album released on Arts and Crafts records in June, and have independently scored short films for directors Clayton Vomero and Larry Cohen.


Alex currently sits as in house producer and engineer for XL Record's NYC studio, and recently mixed Neon Indians album Vega International: Night School.


Ryan has spent the last few years on the road directing Porter Robinson's live visuals and musically directs Brooklyn band Prince Rama's live show.
